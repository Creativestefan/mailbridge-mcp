## 2. What Mailbridge is

Mailbridge is a free, open-source MCP (Model Context Protocol) plugin that connects AI assistants — including Claude (Anthropic), OpenAI Codex, and other MCP-compatible clients — to your personal email account via IMAP and SMTP.

It runs entirely on your local device. There is no Mailbridge cloud service, subscription, or backend server.
